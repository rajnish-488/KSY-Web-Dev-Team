Swayam Yoga day 2021- Event Live here

KhushiSeYoga’s first virtual event “SWAYAM: Exploring Self” was a grand success. In total 616 Yoga enthusiasts learned Yoga from 14 highly experienced and certified Yoga instructors with 8 sponsors who helped to achieve the feat during COVID uncertainties. 

 

The feedback from the participants was positive. Our sessions were watched live by 17K+ people and our posts garnered 6000+ reactions. Watch the yoga session videos on our Facebook page.

 

We would to thank our last year partners: Yogaland, Shemaroo, StepSetGo, Fitternity, Mitti ke Rang, Zymrat, Skillery and sukruthi packaging for supporting us.
